DRESS

Attire for the Journey

The road calls for dressy casual — somewhere between the comfort of the Shire and the formality of a royal court.

Think above jeans, below tuxedos or ball gowns. Polished, intentional, and ready for an outdoor celebration. If you wish to dress further toward the heights of Minas Tirith, you are more than welcome — but no quest attire is required.

Above all: wear something that lets you celebrate freely and stay for the full tale.

A Different Kind of Gift

Your presence is the greatest gift we could ask for.

If you’d like to bring something, we invite you to bring one book that holds meaning to you. Inside the cover, please write a note, a memory, a wish, or a few words of your choosing.
