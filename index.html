DRESS

Dressy Casual

Think of it as a comfortable middle path: above jeans, and below tuxedos and ball gowns — unless you feel like arriving in full regalia, in which case we won’t stop you.

A simple dress, a skirt and blouse, slacks with a button-down, a nice jumpsuit — all welcome. We’re aiming for a fun celebration rather than something stiff or formal.

REGISTRY

No Gifts — One Book

We truly don’t need wedding gifts. If you’d like to bring something, bring one book that means something to you — a story you’ve loved, a book that helped you, or a favorite you’d recommend to a fellow traveler.

Please sign the inside cover with a note (anything you’d like). We’ll build our little library one well-loved volume at a time.
