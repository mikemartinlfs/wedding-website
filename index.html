REGISTRY

A Different Kind of Gift

We are fortunate to already have everything we need for our home and our life together.

Instead of traditional wedding gifts, we invite you to bring one book — a story, a favorite, a well-worn companion, or a quiet classic — something that holds meaning to you.

Inside the cover, please write a note, a memory, a wish, or a few words of your choosing.

These books will become part of our shared library, each one a reminder of the people who walked with us on this journey.

Your presence is the greatest gift we could ask for. This is simply a way to leave a small piece of your story with us.
